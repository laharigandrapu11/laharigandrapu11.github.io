About

I'm a software engineer with about 2.5 years of experience at SAP Labs, where I started as an intern and converted to full-time. My work there was mostly around building automation tools and dashboards that reduced manual effort for both developers and customers. Things that made people's lives easier and saved real time.

While I enjoyed the work, I kept feeling like I wanted to go deeper into AI and understand how to actually build intelligent systems. That's what brought me to Indiana University for my master's in Data Science, where I'm graduating this May.

Since then I've been building at that intersection: LLM-powered agents, computer vision tools, microservice platforms, while also serving as a Teaching Assistant for Vibe & AI Programming and a Research Assistant at IU Libraries.
